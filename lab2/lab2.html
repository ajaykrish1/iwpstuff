1. Labrador Retriever

From jumping into a pool with the kids to enjoying the day out in the great outdoors hiking with his favorite pet parents, with a Labrador Retriever in your home – you’ll always have a willing companion beside you. For more info

2. German Shepherd Dog

The only breed of large dog with the closest, natural resemblance to a wolf is the German Shepherd who has remained one of the top two picks for Americans for many years. Considered a faithful and utterly devoted companion, this pup also fits in perfectly to a home with established pets. For more info

3. Golden Retriever

Highly social, very energetic and known for his great enthusiasm to jump in and take part in whatever is going on – the Golden Retriever has been a firm family favorite for decades. The breed’s naturally cheerful and always-ready-to-do-life-together attitude make Goldens excellent companions, instantly turning anyone new to them into an immediate friend. For more info

4. Standard Poodle

The Poodle’s active personality, love of children and non-shedding coat ticks all the right boxes for an easy-going family. For more info

5. Rottweiler

Looks can be deceptive and that is never truer than when it comes to the Rottweiler. A powerful dog by nature, this medium to large pup is deeply loyal, very affectionate and reserved around new people. For more info

6. German Shorthaired Pointer

Designed for a very active pet parent, German Shorthaired Pointers are a high energy, endurance breed looking to spend almost all day outdoors. An ideal medium dog breed to take trailblazing along with your highly active family, you can guarantee this great dog will enjoy exploring both land and water with you. For more info

7. Great Dane

Best known for their friendly and patient demeanor, the Great Dane may fall into the largest breed of dog category but that doesn’t mean he needs a living space to match his size. This breed can be content in an apartment if there is enough space to stretch out and if regular, short walks are part of his daily routine. For more info
